Success Stories

Customer Relationship Management

Our CRM system provided a one-point portal for several customers, manufacturers, and suppliers to connect and manage their relationships seamlessly. Quality Improvement System

Our Quality Improvement System app helped two manufacturers to track production, record deviations, automate day-to-day reporting, measure key performance indicators, and create a dashboard to monitor performance. This solution improved the quality of the products, reduced production errors, and led to significant cost savings for the clients.

Live Production Dashboard

We developed a Live Production Dashboard for a well-known manufacturing company that provided real-time status updates, automated administrative tasks, a reporting system for operators, and improved monitoring of quality deviations. This solution increased production efficiency and transparency, resulting in a 15% increase in output and reduced downtime for the client.

Player Management System

Our Player Management System for a Cricket board helps in maintaining player records, comparing players, rating players on various skills, selecting and suggesting the best team, adding videos and images from matches and practice. It also provides Spider chart for each player for improvements which resulted in better performance by the team.
